The lineup

Honda Cars Rock Hill

Honda Cars Rock Hill carries the full current Honda lineup, from efficient commuters to family-ready SUVs and minivans. Here are a few of the most popular models Luke sells — and he can build and price any trim for you.

Civic

A sporty, fuel-efficient compact sedan built for daily driving. Great pick for first-time buyers.

  • Up to 32 city / 41 hwy mpg
  • Honda Sensing safety suite standard
  • Sedan or Hybrid available

Accord Hybrid

A midsize sedan with a smooth hybrid powertrain, roomy cabin, and confident highway comfort.

  • Excellent hybrid fuel economy
  • Spacious, quiet interior
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay / Android Auto

CR-V

Honda's best-selling SUV — practical cargo space, available AWD, and a Hybrid option for even better mileage.

  • All-wheel drive available
  • Roomy cargo area
  • Hybrid trim available

Pilot

A three-row family SUV with V6 power, seating for up to 8, and available all-terrain TrailSport capability.

  • Seats up to 8
  • 285-hp V6 engine
  • Up to 5,000 lb towing

HR-V

A compact crossover that's easy to park with clever interior storage — a favorite for first-time SUV buyers.

  • Compact, easy to park
  • Available all-wheel drive
  • Great value for the price

Odyssey

Honda's minivan — a strong V6, a long list of standard features, and more real-world family space than most SUVs.

  • Seats up to 8
  • Loaded with standard features
  • Best-in-class family versatility

Trims, availability, and features change often — call Luke for current pricing and what's on the lot.
